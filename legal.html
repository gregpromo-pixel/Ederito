Maintenance policy

What the six included months actually cover

When expressly included in the signed project documents, the six-month period begins on the earlier of production launch or client acceptance. It covers reasonable bug fixes attributable to the delivered build, routine dependency or security updates when practical, basic monitoring, recovery from available Ederito-managed backups, and limited small content edits within the original scope.

It does not include new features, redesigns, new pages, business-rule changes, migrations, third-party failures, app-store work, emergency after-hours work, malware caused by client or third-party access, content creation, SEO campaigns, hosting/domain/vendor charges or work on systems modified by others. Unused support does not roll over or convert to cash. Continued support after expiration requires a paid plan or separate quote.
