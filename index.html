About Eder

Business-minded. Design-led. Grounded in service.

I’m Eder Thermozier, a designer, developer and entrepreneur from Léogâne, Haiti. I earned a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration from the University of the Ozarks, where I developed a deeper understanding of business, technology, communication and leadership.

My work is also shaped by service. Alongside my twin brother and friends, I helped create BLEUET, an initiative supporting disadvantaged children in Haiti through scholarships, school resources, hygiene kits and community assistance. That same purpose drives how I build: create useful things, help people move forward, and leave a meaningful impact.
